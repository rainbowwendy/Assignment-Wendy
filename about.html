About US

The very first home cleaned by Sunflower Cleaning was in Mississauga, Ontario, by our founders Wendy Lee back in 2022.
The Stringers believed that busy families and homeowners would rather spend their time focusing on the more important
things in life, rather than cleaning their home. It didn’t take long for Sunflower Cleaning to become a household name
in Mississauga and the surrounding area. One reason was because of our easily identifiable pink and blue corporate colours.
In fact, even today many people recognize Sunflower Cleaning by the distinctive colours of our vehicles and by our commitment
to providing the highest quality house cleaning possible.Since 2010, Sunflower Cleaning has expanded not only across Canada
but also into the United States, the United Kingdom, Japan and Portugal. Each year, Sunflower Cleaning Franchises perform
more than 2 million home cleanings — all from our humble beginnings in Mississauga, Ontario. We’re proud of our history and
of our founders, Adrienne and Chris, for starting what has become one of the world’s most successful residential cleaning
companies.
